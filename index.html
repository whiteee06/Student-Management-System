📚 About Us

Department of AI & Data Science

Empowering students with cutting-edge knowledge in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

💡

Vision

To be a nationally recognized center of excellence in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, producing innovative leaders who drive technological advancement for societal benefit.

🎯

Mission

To provide world-class education in AI and Data Science through innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and industry collaboration, nurturing creative problem-solvers.

✅

Objectives

To equip students with practical AI skills, foster research mindset, promote interdisciplinary learning, and build industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values.
